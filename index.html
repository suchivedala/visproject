What is this?

A multidimensional explorer of Top 500 Grossing Apps of data from Droid Meter.

Controls

Brush: Drag vertically along an axis.
Remove Brush: Tap the axis background.
Reorder Axes: Drag a label horizontally.
Invert Axis: Tap an axis label.
Remove Axis: Drag axis label to left or right edge.
